Why Albania?

Albania, the perfect vacation!

Head to a country brimming with a glorious history, spectacular nature, and bursting cultural wonders that will leave you speechless. Albania offers everything you wish for a perfect vacation. Shining its own light, Albania is an incredible hidden gem in Europe.

Nothing looks as wonderful as Albania’s nature. It displays a picturesque landscape on both land and water. Find swirling mountains and snow-coated peaks in the spectacular Albanian Alps. Verdant meadows and misty streams adorn the mountains’ foothills, creating an unforgettable scene for everyone who visits. Meanwhile, the southern part of Albania becomes the meeting point of the Ionian and Adriatic Seas. Flawless, powdery beaches stretch along the land, offering a perfect sunbathing spot. Get ready to be captivated by the popping colors of the brilliant blue ocean and the white-tinted coastline. Therefore, travel to Albania to see these exquisite scenes with your own eyes!

Must-see attractions

My favorite places in Albania

Butrint

Early in the morning, before the tourist crowds arrive and when the rocks are still tinged in the yellow dawn light, you might just imagine that the ancient walls of Butrint are whispering secrets to you of long-past lives. Easily the most romantic and beautiful – not to mention largest – of Albania's ancient sites, Butrint, 18km south of Saranda, is worth travelling a long way to see.

Blue Eye Spring

The Blue Eye Spring is a magical place: a hypnotic pool of deep blue water surrounded by electric-blue edges like the iris of an eye. It's further enveloped in thick woods and is some 22km east of Saranda on the road to Gjirokastra.

Address:

The blue eye, Albania

Saranda

Saranda is a beautiful coastal city in Albania that offers a range of attractions and activities for travelers of all interests. From exploring historic sites to enjoying the beach and the sea, there’s no shortage of things to do in Saranda. Whether you’re a history buff, a beach lover, or an adventure seeker, Saranda has something to offer. .
